Dal Makhni

Dal Makhani is a popular North Indian dish made from black lentils (urad dal) and kidney beans (rajma), cooked with butter, cream, and a blend of spices. It's typically simmered for hours to achieve a rich, creamy texture and is often served with rice or Indian breads like naan or roti. The dish has a luxurious and indulgent flavor profile due to the use of butter and cream, making it a favorite in Indian cuisine, especially during special occasions and celebrations.

Fried Rice

Vegetable fried rice is a popular dish in many Asian cuisines, particularly Chinese cuisine. It's made by stir-frying cooked rice with a variety of vegetables such as carrots, peas, bell peppers, onions, and sometimes additional ingredients like mushrooms or broccoli. The dish is typically seasoned with soy sauce, garlic, ginger, and other spices to enhance the flavor. It's a versatile and customizable dish that can be enjoyed on its own or as a side dish with other Asian dishes like stir-fries or curries.

Pav Bhaji

Pav bhaji is a fast food dish from India consisting of a thick vegetable curry (bhaji) served with a soft bread roll (pav). Its origins are in the state of Maharashtra. Pav bhaji is a spiced mixture of mashed vegetables in a thick gravy served with bread.

Paneer Butter Masala

Paneer Butter Masala is a classic vegetarian dish from North India. It consists of paneer (Indian cottage cheese) cooked in a rich and creamy tomato-based gravy, flavored with butter, cream, and a blend of aromatic spices such as cumin, coriander, and garam masala. The dish is known for its luscious texture and vibrant flavors, making it a popular choice in Indian restaurants and households alike. It's often served with rice or Indian breads like naan or roti.
